General Questions

What is TymblHub?

TymblHub is a professional referral platform that connects job seekers with referrers inside companies. We streamline the referral process to help candidates get noticed and hired faster.

How does TymblHub work?

Job seekers create profiles and apply to positions. If your skills match, we connect you with employees who can refer you internally. Employers can post jobs and find qualified candidates through trusted referrals.

Is TymblHub available internationally?

Yes! TymblHub is available worldwide. Our platform supports job seekers and employers from any country, with a growing network of referrers across global companies.

For Job Seekers

Is TymblHub completely free for job seekers?

TymblHub offers a free Basic plan with limited applications per month. For unlimited applications and premium features, we offer a Pro plan at ₹99/month. You can start with the free plan and upgrade anytime.

How do I increase my chances of getting a referral?

Complete your profile with relevant skills and experience, apply to positions that match your qualifications, and follow the skill enhancement suggestions we provide. A detailed and accurate profile significantly improves your referral chances.

What happens after I get referred?

Once referred, your application receives priority in the company's hiring process. You'll receive real-time status updates and may be contacted directly by the company's recruiters for next steps if they're interested.

For Employers

How does TymblHub benefit employers?

TymblHub provides access to pre-vetted candidates through trusted referrals, which typically yield higher quality hires with better cultural fit and retention rates. Our platform reduces time-to-hire and recruitment costs significantly.

What does the Enterprise plan include?

Our Enterprise plan includes unlimited job postings, company branding, advanced candidate filtering, an applicant tracking system, hiring analytics, and API access for integration with your existing HR systems. Contact our sales team for custom pricing.

How do we encourage our employees to become referrers?

TymblHub makes it easy for your employees to refer candidates with minimal effort. Many companies also implement internal incentive programs alongside our platform to reward successful referrals.

Subscription & Billing

How do I upgrade from the free plan?

To upgrade, go to your account settings and select "Subscription". Choose the Pro plan for ₹99/month or contact us for Enterprise options. We accept all major credit cards and UPI payments.

Is there a discount for annual billing?

Yes! You can save up to 20% by choosing annual billing. The Pro plan is available for ₹949/year (equivalent to ₹79/month) instead of the monthly rate of ₹99/month.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. Your benefits will continue until the end of your current billing period with no additional charges.
